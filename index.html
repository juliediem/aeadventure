The Rules

1. One (1) ballot is awarded when deals equivalent to $10,000 USD are closed; For every $10,000 increment you hit in total SOW revenue closed, you get one ballot. For example, if you bring in a single deal/multiple deals worth $20,000 in total you get (2) ballots. If you bring in a single/multiple deals that equal $10,000, you will receive one (1) ballot.

2. All SOW's must be closed on or before January 31st, 2018 at midnight EST

3.Any deals currently open right now and closed starting today until January 31st, 2018 midnight EST will be counted towards the draw.

4. Each ballot will be entered into a random draw program that will select a winner. The winner will be announced in a mass email to all AEs, and the winner will also receive a personal email containing details on next steps.

5. Only 1 of the 3 adventures will be awarded, winner will get to select which adventure they would like to go on.

6. The Foodie Adventure will be the only adventure where you cannot select your own dates. Due to high demand at Michelin starred restaurants, there is often a month long waitlist for some. You will be awarded your flight vouchers as soon as you pick your prize, and we will be letting you know as soon as possible when your dinner reservations are secured and paid for.

7. Applicable to every adventure: for the 2 round trip flights, you will be awarded a gift card with the average cost of a flight. It will be your responsibility to book your flights as appropriate. You will be awarded a giftcard for the specified prizes that you can redeem.
